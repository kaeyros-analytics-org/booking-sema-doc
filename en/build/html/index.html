Appointment Booking

Booking refers to all the actions required to book a service with a practitioner via the platform. It involves a series of steps from collecting client information to confirming the appointment, including selecting the service, host, and time slot. This process can be initiated by a user via the chatbot or by a receptionist from the management interface.
